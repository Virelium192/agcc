Privacy Policy

Your data protection and privacy rights are fundamental to our mission of empowering farmers worldwide.

Last Updated: December 15, 2024
Reading Time: 15 minutes

Quick Summary

AgriClimate Connect is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. We collect minimal necessary information to provide our agricultural knowledge services, never sell your data to third parties, and give you full control over your information. This policy explains our practices in clear, understandable language.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when using our agricultural knowledge platform:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Farm location and agricultural interests
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Communication preferences
  • Support requests and feedback

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your interaction with our platform:

  • Pages visited and features used
  • Time spent on different sections
  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and general location
  • Agricultural content preferences and learning progress

Technical Information

To ensure optimal performance of our agricultural tools and AI-powered features, we collect technical data such as system performance metrics, error logs, and feature usage analytics. This helps us improve our services and provide better farming insights.

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery

Your information helps us provide personalized agricultural knowledge and tools:

  • Customize weather and climate recommendations for your region
  • Provide relevant crop and livestock management advice
  • Deliver market intelligence suited to your farming interests
  • Enable AI-powered tools and predictive analytics
  • Track your learning progress and achievements

Communication

We use your contact information to send important updates about agricultural conditions, new knowledge resources, platform improvements, and respond to your support requests. You can control these communications through your account settings.

Service Improvement

We analyze usage patterns to enhance our agricultural knowledge platform, develop new features that benefit farmers, improve AI accuracy, and ensure the reliability of our weather and market data services.

3. Information Sharing

Our Commitment

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your agricultural data and farming insights remain confidential and are used solely to improve your experience on our platform.

Limited Sharing Scenarios

We only share your information in these specific circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who help operate our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your explicit consent for specific partnerships or collaborations
  • In anonymized, aggregated form for agricultural research that benefits the farming community

Trusted Partners

We work with reputable agricultural organizations, weather data providers, and technology partners who share our commitment to farmer privacy. All partnerships are governed by strict data protection agreements that align with our privacy standards.

4. Data Security

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your agricultural data:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure data storage with regular backups
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Staff training on data protection and privacy practices

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we have established procedures to quickly identify, contain, and resolve the issue. We will notify affected users within 72 hours and provide clear information about the steps being taken to protect their data.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Essential Cookies

We use necessary cookies to ensure our agricultural platform functions properly. These include session management, security features, language preferences, accessibility settings, and your personalized farming dashboard configuration.

Analytics Cookies

With your consent, we use analytics cookies to understand how farmers use our knowledge tools, which features are most helpful, and how we can improve the agricultural learning experience. This data helps us develop better farming resources.

Your Control

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent panel. Disabling certain cookies may limit some platform functionality, but core agricultural knowledge features will remain available.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Access & Control

You have comprehensive rights regarding your agricultural data and personal information:

  • Access and download all your personal data and farming preferences
  • Correct or update any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Port your data to another agricultural platform
  • Object to certain data processing activities
  • Request restriction of data processing

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact our privacy team at privacy@agriclimateconnect.com. We will respond within 30 days and verify your identity to protect your data. Most actions can also be performed directly through your account settings.

7. Third-Party Services

Weather & Climate Data

We integrate with trusted weather services to provide accurate agricultural forecasts. These services may use your general location to deliver relevant climate information, but they do not receive your personal identification details.

Payment Processing

Payment transactions for premium agricultural courses and tools are processed by certified third-party payment processors. We do not store your payment information on our servers, and all transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.

External Links

Our platform may contain links to external agricultural resources, government agencies, and educational institutions. These websites have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them before sharing personal information.

8. International Users

Global Agricultural Community

AgriClimate Connect serves farmers worldwide, and we comply with international data protection regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws. Your data may be processed in different countries to provide optimal agricultural services.

Data Transfers

When we transfer data internationally for agricultural services, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses, adequacy decisions, and certification schemes that provide equivalent protection to your home country's privacy laws.

Local Privacy Laws

If you are located in a region with specific privacy laws (such as the EU, California, or Canada), you may have additional rights under those laws. Please contact us to learn more about rights specific to your jurisdiction.

9. Children's Privacy

Age Requirement

Our agricultural knowledge platform is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Young Farmers Program

For our educational programs aimed at young farmers (ages 13-17), we require parental or guardian consent and take additional measures to protect young users:

  • Limited data collection focused on educational needs
  • Enhanced privacy controls and parental oversight
  • Restricted communication features
  • Regular review of accounts by our education team

10. Data Retention

Active Accounts

We retain your agricultural data and learning progress while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward to provide continuous farming insights and allow for account recovery. Your data helps us improve seasonal predictions and agricultural recommendations.

Inactive Accounts

If you don't use your account for 3 years, we will contact you about account status. After 4 years of inactivity, we may delete your account and associated data, except for information we are legally required to retain for agricultural reporting or regulatory purposes.

Legal Requirements

Some agricultural data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, or support agricultural research that benefits the farming community. This includes anonymized crop yield data and climate adaptation insights.

11. Privacy Policy Updates

Policy Changes

As agricultural technology evolves and privacy regulations develop, we may need to update this privacy policy. We are committed to maintaining transparency about how we protect farmer data and will clearly communicate any changes that affect your privacy rights.

Change Notifications

For significant changes to our privacy practices, we will notify you at least 30 days in advance via email, platform notifications, or prominent website notices. Minor updates will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Continued Use

Your continued use of AgriClimate Connect after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms. If you disagree with changes, you may discontinue using our services and request deletion of your data.
