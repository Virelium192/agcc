Our Purpose & Values

Built on a foundation of innovation, sustainability, and farmer empowerment, we're transforming agriculture for a better tomorrow.

18

Foundation

2018

Founded by Dr. Maria Gonzalez and Prof. James Chen with a vision to democratize agricultural knowledge. Started with 3 team members in Silicon Valley, focusing on climate-smart farming solutions.

19

First Platform Launch

2019

Launched our first web platform with weather intelligence and crop advisory features. Reached 10,000 farmers across 12 countries. Secured Series A funding of $5M.

20

AI Integration

2020

Introduced AI-powered crop health analysis and predictive analytics. Partnership with leading agricultural universities. User base grew to 100,000 farmers globally.

21

Global Expansion

2021

Expanded to 75 countries with localized content in 15 languages. Launched livestock management and market intelligence modules. Reached 500,000 active users.

22

Global Recognition

2022

Named Social Impact Leader by World Economic Forum. Achieved carbon-neutral certification. Launched community marketplace connecting 1M+ farmers globally.

23

Innovation Leadership

2023

Won Best Agricultural Innovation Award. Launched advanced AI tools with 98% accuracy. Serving 2.5M farmers across 150 countries. Series C funding of $50M.

24

Continuing Innovation

2024

Launching next-generation platform with advanced climate modeling, IoT integration, and blockchain-based supply chain solutions. Target: 5M farmers by 2025.
